## About Me

I'm a passionate historian, educator, and social data scientist who believes in the power of storytelling through data. Deeply committed to social justice and LGBTQ+ rights, I've made it my mission to bridge the gap between advocacy and education through data. When I'm not crunching numbers, speaking, or teaching, you'll find me behind the microphone hosting my podcast [Trans* Lesson Plan](https://open.spotify.com/show/1C8sCmMmmlPSsguU9XuOHp?si=zOVtuaEUTcygJBkz321amA). I'm proficient in Python, R, and Power BI for data analysis and visualization tools that help me create compelling narratives from datasets. This technical foundation enables me to process large amounts of social data and create accessible visualizations that illuminate key insights about intersectional experiences and create real-world change. I bring my whole self to every project, combining technical expertise with my passion for social justice. Through my work, I aim to push boundaries and tell our community's stories in powerful new ways.
